Project Description

Battlelog Mobile retrieves your real time Battlefield 3 soldier information from EA / DICE Battlelog server to your Windows Phone. You are able to track your progression in easy, intuitive manner with lots of images and charts.

All three platforms are supported (PC, PS3, XBox).

Battlelog Mobile is a open source project it it has nothing to do with official EA's Battlefield 3 or Battlelog products.

Status

Version 1.5.0.0 is available for download from Marketplace and version 1.6.0.0 is out really soon, fixing issues raised by Armored Kill DLC release. See the Issue Tracker if you want to know what is being developed for next release. You are also welcome to contact us via email or Battlelog.
